Introduction

Given the current situation and the need for agility needed to provide you with a great learning experience despite all the challenges, we will use this site as the full repository of course materials. We will also use Slack for communication during and after the course. Let's make this happen!

Course Topics and Learning Materials

During the course we will use this section to point to course materials from the book and upload any extra course content. We will build this repository as the course progresses. Upon completion of the semester this website will become the record of and source for all you need to complete the course.

Book and Learning Materials

The book is avaialble for purchase directly from the publisher in both electronic and paper format. There are two versions, one with full cases and one without.

We will also have some additional required reading material on hardware, software and netweorking foundational concepts. To view the material you need to log in.


Other required and optional learning materials will be linked from this online repository.
